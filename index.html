Reverse Engineering

Hanaglobal is utilizing many years of hands on experience to provide our customers with a full commitment of fast and efficient reverse engineering services.
Starting with only your existing physical part we use 3D scanning technologies. Such as contact and Non-Contact probe with Enhanced Scanning Technology to determine the objects dimensional and geometric characteristics.
Hanaglobal Designer then uses the cloud point data along with latest software applications to process the line and point or point cloud data into a more usable customer specified 3D CAD model format.
